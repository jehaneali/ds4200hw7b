Average Flow by Time Period

This visualization depicts the average flow for each time period that's tracked. Interestingly, the time periods with the lowest and highest amounts of flow are right next to each other -- time period 9 has an average flow of 84, whereas time period 10 has an average flow of 7319.

Total Ons vs Total Offs

This visualizations depics the total ons versus total offs for all points in the dataset. The most surprising thing was how different the scales for ons vs offs was -- total ons actually got past 100,000 in some cases, while total offs had few past 10 or 11 thousand.

The pop out effect I used was highlighting the rides that were on the orange or green line in their corresponding color. I chose this approach because I thought that it was the most intuitive, and that orange and green would really pop out against a backdrop of gray points, which represent the red and blue lines.
